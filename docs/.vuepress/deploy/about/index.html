# Howdy Partner! 😻

Thanks for participating in the 2023 scavanger hunt 🎉. Before you get started please scroll down and review the instructions and guidelines below. 👓...

TIP

THERE WILL BE PRIZES! for the winners 🏆 🥇 🥈 🥉

# Instructions 📷 🎥

Take pictures and short videos to earn points. From the menu select a category to see what points you can earn! Submit your pictures and videos via email to jrieger@berkleyentertainment.com or via SMS to 713-504-7003.

# Guidelines 📚

  • 🌞 Don't forget to HAVE FUN!
  • Please send each of your submissions with the category and item #️⃣ or item title.
  • Submission limits - Only 10 items per participant! Participants can submit photos/videos for 10 of the 25 items from the five categories.
  • Obey all applicable laws while participating...
    • No trespassing - Don't stray into restricted areas in your quest for points.
    • No graffiti - Don't damage or deface property while participating.
